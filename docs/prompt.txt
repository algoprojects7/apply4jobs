# PROJECT TITLE

LeadHire AI – Intelligent AI-Powered Job Marketplace Platform

---

# PROJECT OVERVIEW

Design and develop a highly scalable, enterprise-grade, AI-powered Job Marketplace Platform where:

1. Employers can create company profiles and post jobs.
2. Job seekers can create profiles, upload resumes, search jobs, and apply instantly.
3. AI continuously analyzes candidate behavior and recommends highly relevant jobs.
4. AI analyzes uploaded resumes and provides professional resume improvement suggestions.
5. AI learns candidate search behavior and continuously improves recommendations.
6. AI recommends candidates to employers.
7. AI provides skill-gap analysis and career growth recommendations.
8. System must support millions of users with minimal latency.
9. Platform must be developed as a Multi-Tenant SaaS Application.
